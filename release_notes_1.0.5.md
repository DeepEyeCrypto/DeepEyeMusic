# DeepEyeMusicPro v1.0.5

## What's New
- **Polished Update Popup**: The in-app update popup has been completely redesigned with a modern, glass-morphic aesthetic, animated icons, and better readability.
- **Profile Picture Fix**: The Settings screen now correctly displays your Google profile picture inside a stylish glowing ring avatar.
- **Codebase Cleanups**: Massive under-the-hood optimization to eliminate over 30 deprecation warnings. Improved app stability and compatibility with newer Android versions.

> **Installation Note:** If Google Play Protect shows an "App scan recommended" warning during installation, this is standard behavior for apps installed outside the Play Store. It is completely safe to click **"More details"** -> **"Install anyway"**.
